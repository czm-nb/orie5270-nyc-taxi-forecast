Taxi zone polygons (taxi_zones.geojson)
- Derived from the NYC TLC shapefile distributed with Yellow Taxi documentation:
  https://d37ci6vzurychx.cloudfront.net/misc/taxi_zones.zip
- Processed locally: simplified geometry, rounded coordinates, and filtered to the
  five NYC boroughs (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, Staten Island) for map UX.
  Zones outside this filter (e.g. EWR) are not drawn but can still be typed by ID if needed.
